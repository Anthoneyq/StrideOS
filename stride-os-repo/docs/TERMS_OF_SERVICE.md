## What STRIDE OS Is

STRIDE OS is a pacing calculator and training reference tool for running coaches. It uses published sport science formulas to estimate race times and prescribe training paces.

**It is a decision support tool, not a coaching service.**

---

## What You're Responsible For

By using STRIDE OS, you agree that:

1. **You make the final coaching decisions.** STRIDE OS provides reference paces and analysis. Your coaching judgment and athlete context always override anything the app suggests.

2. **You know your athletes.** You understand their age, training age, injury history, and individual needs. The app gives general guidance; you apply it with your knowledge of each athlete.

3. **You're responsible for athlete safety.** STRIDE OS is not a medical tool. It doesn't assess injury risk, detect overtraining, or manage health conditions. If an athlete is injured, fatigued, or unwell, your coaching decision — not the app — determines training.

4. **You have authorization to enter athlete data.** For athletes under 13, you confirm you have parental consent. For athletes at schools, you confirm you have authority to enter their information.

---

## What STRIDE OS Is NOT

STRIDE OS does not:

- Replace coaching judgment
- Diagnose or treat injuries
- Assess athlete health or readiness
- Predict injury or illness
- Make psychological evaluations
- Provide medical advice
- Guarantee race performance

## Data You Provide

When you use STRIDE OS, you may provide:

- Athlete names and demographics
- Race results and times
- Training notes and observations
- Recovery data (sleep, heart rate, mood)

**You own your data.** You can export it, delete it, or ask us to delete it at any time.

**We don't sell your data.** We keep aggregate (anonymized) summaries to improve the app's accuracy, but we don't share individual athlete information with third parties without your explicit consent.

## For Coaches With Athletes Under 13

Federal law (COPPA) requires parental consent before we collect data on children under 13.

**Before you create a profile for an athlete under 13:**
- You must have written parental permission
- Parents must understand what data is being collected
- We will ask you to confirm you have this permission

We take this seriously. If we discover you've entered data for under-13 athletes without parental consent, we will delete their profiles.
