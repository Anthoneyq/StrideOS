About Critical Velocity: CV is the fastest pace you can sustain all-out for approximately 30 minutes. It sits between threshold and VO2max intensity, training your Type IIa muscle fibers to be oxygen consumers and power producers. This improves race pace sustainability and kicking power across all distances from 800m to marathon.

Sample CV Workouts

  • 📍 Easy CV Builder: 4-5 × 1000m @ CV pace with 90-120s easy jog recovery
  • 📍 Medium CV Session: 6-7 × 800m @ CV pace with 90s recovery
  • 📍 Long CV Push: 4-6 × 1.5-2km @ CV pace with 2-3min recovery
  • 📍 CV + Speed: 5 × (600m @ CV, 200m @ mile race pace) with full recovery
  • 📍 CV Pyramid: 400, 800, 1200, 1000, 800, 400m all @ CV with 60-90s jog
